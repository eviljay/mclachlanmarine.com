Why Choose Us
We have quayside offices at both Aberdeen & Montrose harbours with our main Aberdeenshire Office, Workshops & 1/2 acre yard situated between Aberdeen - Dundee, this puts us in the prime location to cater for East of Scotland projects with superior infrastructure than that of the competition.
We have a wealth of maritime experience, specialist vessels, excellent approach to safety, and friendly team. 
We have built a large diverse customer base from all over the UK. 
Some of our regular work includes equipment sea trials, pipeline, seabed surveys, safety boats and construction support.

Safety
All our coxswain's & crews have industry experience and trained to MCA & RYA standards.  Before stepping aboard you will be provided with an automatic lifejacket then given a short safety brief on the safety equipment and safety procedures.  Our MCA Workboat certification is available for inspection.
Our safety management system is approved and registered to ISO9001 & ISO14001.
