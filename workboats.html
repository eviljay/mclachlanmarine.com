Workboat Services Scotland
Providing workboat & Safety boat hire since 2011 for the testing and calibrating of sonar equipment, hydrographic surveys and crew transfers, from our base in Aberdeen, Scotland.

Variety of Work:
With a variety of projects from offshore windfarms to harbour construction MM vessels have supported the many stages from cable lay, dive teams, trenching, foundations, mooring and dredging. McLachlan Marine have a wealth of experience in these sectors.

With our Aberdeen NE Scotland base and infrastructure many of our clients ask us to take a key role in the project supplying marine personnel, project managers, warehousing and local consultancy/ liaison with port authorities.
