Welcome

McLachlan Marine is one of the largest independent workboat operators in North East of Scotland, based in Aberdeen owning and operating a growing fleet of over 10 Workboats.
Established in 2011, operating as Castle Charter & Marine Services Ltd, trading as McLachlan Marine, the growth of the business has expanded into new markets.

With a diverse profile of projects, services and quayside base at Aberdeen Harbour we provide crew launch, crew transfers, workboat hire, safety boats, seabed survey boats, equipment sea trials and equipment deployments around Scotland.

Our fantastic operations team have a diverse wealth of experience from offshore, oil & gas, renewables, shipping, lifeboats & maritime training.
Key Aberdeen Services
  • 9x Workboats for hire Scotland
  • 3x Inshore Survey Boats
  • 1x Offshore Survey Boat
  • 4x Safety Boats for hire
  • Oil Spill Response
  • Marine Project Support
  • Crew Transfer CTV's
  • ROV Services
  • Logistics & Quayside Personnel

We take pride in offering quality, safe & competitive marine support solutions.
